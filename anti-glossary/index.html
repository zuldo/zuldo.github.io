Anti-glossary

Like the glossary, except instead of constructing useful concepts, here I'm deconstructing harmful concepts.

Snowflake

A way of punishing people for experiencing and/or expressing preferences/desires/[things they care about], and associated negative valence. Example:

Alice: as a child I used to go out of my way to make it hard for my grandfather to throw clams at me, because I was scared of clams.

Bob: snowflake.

- Example taken from this.

The sentiment expressed by the term seems to be roughly "stop caring about things that I don't want you to care about.".

Antitransists often use this term to punish trans people for various things, such as not pretending to be cis, or being gender non-conforming.
