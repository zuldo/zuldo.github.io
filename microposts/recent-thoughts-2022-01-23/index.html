recent-thoughts-2022-01-23

Recent insights, realizations, ideas, observations, things I want to keep in mind

  • "do not ruminate on things you can't control" is pretty good advice
    • how to apply in practice?
      • probably helps a bit to actually accurately gauge how much influence you have over a situation
  • lots of singers, voice actors, and trans people learn unusually much about using their voice. what about relearning how to use other things taken for granted like voice is?
    • walking
    • relaxing
    • dealing with emotions
    • making friends
      • keeping friends
  • you're allowed to both X and be afraid of not-X
    • in general, you're allowed to be afraid
    • and to do things without justifying them
  • communities are cool
  • having people directly addressing me in group conversations feels nice
  • no complaining challenge: don't complain about anything for a period of time
    • means you're pushed to either drop feelings of dissatisfaction or act on them
  • the more time i spend in solitude the more interesting thoughts i have that may lead to interesting conversations
