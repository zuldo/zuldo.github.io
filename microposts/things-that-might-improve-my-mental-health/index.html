Things that might improve my mental health

Some things that might improve my mental health

These are observations from my memory and notes of activities that potentially improve my mental health.

  • [redacted]
    • kind of "do what you want"
    • probably related to replacing guilt
      • kind of lets me only feel bad about doing things that i genuinely regret
  • gratitude journaling
    • i just enter date and hour (if changed since last entry) then short descriptions of things i'm grateful of
    • loose definition of "gratitude"; can include e.g. things i'm proud of. it's less "gratitude journaling", more "healthy & happy stuff journaling"
    • no forcing myself. if i don't want to make an entry i don't make one
  • pushing myself to talk with people
    • not too much. only when it feels like a good idea
  • having inspiring conversations
    • i've gotten lots of this from joining a certain community
  • internal double crux
    • most internal conflicts are probably not fundamentally contradictions of (terminal) values
      • see also Preferences and value drift
  • trying to "make myself" feel happy from friends being happy when i don't automatically
    • might help to explicitly wonder stuff like "what is bad about this situation?" or "what would be better than this?"
  • being less afraid of not-actually-value-drift
  • asking friends if they will tell me if i'm hurting them (and them answering that they will)
  • reconnecting with people i haven't talked with in a while
  • doing the Values exercise
    • i think the process was more impactful than having the result, at least so far
  • reading about several things:
    • craving
    • grasping
    • mental mountains
  • "doing nothing"
    • as in computing
    • as in solitude
  • making cool stuff
    • writing bash script automating various parts of using this blog
    • drawing
  • more conversations in public/non-private spaces
    • can jump in and out of conversations
    • can talk with several people at once
    • makes your relationships legible to other people
  • figuring out more about how i get attracted in various ways to people
    • in the broad sense of "attracted" which includes stuff like platonic and aesthetic attraction
  • started reading "The Courage to Be Disliked"
