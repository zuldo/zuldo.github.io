In Is X "cheating"? I ask:

What game are you playing? [...]

Answer that question, then figure out what the rules are, and you'll know whether you're cheating.

I want to make it explicit that I intended for that post to make you realize that you're probably sometimes thinking as if you're playing a game even though you're not. And sometimes there is a game but it's just implied not explicit, or it's incoherent.
