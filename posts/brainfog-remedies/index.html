Remedies for brain fog

Summary

Collection of methods I know for reducing brain fog.

<todo: define brain fog.>

Advice that is extremely general to the point where it's almost always a good idea and requires basically no thought put into it is preceded by a star "*".

  • *Drink water.
    • It is really hard to drink too much water, and really easy to drink too little. This is almost always a good idea to do.
  • *Breathe fresh air.
    • Same as with the water, except I doubt that "breathing too much fresh air" even makes sense.
  • *Stim/stretch.
    • Since you can do this on the spot (unless you're in a hostile social environment) it's practical to build TAP:s and cogstrats with it.
  • Eat.
