Honest signalling

Summary

A part of being genuine that's basically "let people observe what you actually are without messing with the data."

I want people to believe that I infrequently break promises. What is a reliable way of making this happen?

  1. Infrequently break promises.
  2. Let people know when I'm keeping and not keeping promises without tampering with the data.

Generally, honest signalling is done by letting people observe a part of you(r life) as it is without bias.

To honestly signal something you must also honestly signal its inverse. For instance, letting people see that you're keeping their secrets requires you to also let them see when you are not keeping their secrets.

This works better the more honest you are in general ("If you once tell a lie, the truth is ever after your enemy.")

Keep in mind that you can be honest and still have secrets; simply be honest about that fact.
