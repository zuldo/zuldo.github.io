Mental delta

The difference between a thought and the next.

Examples:

  • See person -> retrieve their name from memory.
  • Notice that an improvement would take less than 10 seconds to implement -> implement it.
  • Experience felt sense -> do Focusing on it.

(These examples aren't very good, because neither is my skill at noticing these (yet).)

(Concept stolen from Be Well Tuned's Tune Your Cognitive Strategies.)

Mental delta reinforcement

Rewarding/reinforcing a mental delta, a la Tune Your Cognitive Strategies.

Empathy

Hardware, or virtualization of such hardware, in human brains, that specialize in modelling other humans. Often extends partially to nonhumans as well. Probably also occurs in some other species. Often has certain mental patterns leaking through from the model to the human using it, especially emotions. Probably emulation, not simulation.

If mirror neurons exist in humans empathy uses them.

Compassion is a pattern of usage of the output of empathy.
