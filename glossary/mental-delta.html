Mental delta

The difference between a thought and the next.

Examples:

  • See person -> retrieve their name from memory.
  • Notice that an improvement would take less than 10 seconds to implement -> implement it.
  • Experience felt sense -> do Focusing on it.

(These examples aren't very good, because neither is my skill at noticing these (yet).)

(Concept stolen from Be Well Tuned's Tune Your Cognitive Strategies.)
